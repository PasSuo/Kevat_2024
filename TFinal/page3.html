How to get started?

Find a place

Google your surroundings. There are several places around the world!

Climbing gyms are different so visit different places.

Start easy

Start with easy boulders and see what grades suit you best.

Don't get stressed if you don't manage the hard ones.

Invite a friend

Or go solo. Climbing can be very social if you decide so.

Experienced climbers are very helpful if you feel stuck or need a hint for a problem.

Get up there!!
